FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We get lots of question daily pertaining gaming in all it's contents.
These are the most frequently asked question we have recieved from the TGB army.

1. What game are you playing right now?

Right now I am enjoy some competitive Dragonball FighterZ. Season 3 just got really interesting with all kinds of changes and development in terms of gameplay in the last update (better know as Season 3.5) that it is blowing the minds of the whole fighting games community.

The DBFZ community is huge and really fun and meeting new and all kinds of people is what keeps me in this game as well as a huge competitive nature. I would recommend this game to anyone wanting to get into the fighting game scene.

2. What or who got you into video games in the first place?

Well, because I grew up in a tough community, my parents always wanted to keep me inside the house. and enter my first ever gaming console, the PS2. Since then, I have never looked back, playing the ps3 as well as the ps4 and currently working hard to continue my tradition by obtaining the ps5 which is another beast on its own.

I am a huge fan of the Playstation for what it has kept me from and I would continue supporting the franchise.

3. What is the longest amount of time you have sat playing games non-stop?

This is a question lots of gamers recieve on a daily basis.

however the longest I have ever played a game non-stop was a 1 full day and the game was, The Last of Us.
From the moment i picked it up, I was just intreged. Filled with suspense and action, the Last of Us alwasy kept me on the edge of my seat. I would recommend this to every gamer out there. One of the best storyline and intense gameplay of its time.

4. What is your favorite game from your childhood?

There was so many! Growing up there is no way I can't mention the likes of Jack 3 or Ratchet and Clank and Prince of persia which will always be one of my all time favourite games.

Before there was any kind of walkthroughs and youtube to make this abit easier. My friends and I would sit for hours on trying to complete a specific section. We would sit for days on a particular part of the and nt make any progress. I still get nightmares of the Dahaka chasing me in Prince of Persia 3.

5. What's your favorite game of all time?

I personally dont have 1 favorite game, lots of different games gives me all kinds of good memories, even the likes of Smackdown vs Raw 2006 gives me great memories.

At the moment, God of War 4 (AKA God of War) is one of the best games I have ever played. As someone that loves action, RPG and a great story, God of War ticks all the boxes. Actually I think I will replay the GOW. Its that good.
